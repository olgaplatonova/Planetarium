large star hall

The starry hall of the planetarium is equipped with the latest model of the Skymaster ZKP4 with a full-dome Spasegate projection system from Carl Zeiss, which consists of six digital independent projectors. The joint control system allows for a smooth transition from analog to digital projection, making optimal use of the advantages of each system.
